My Hobbies & Interests

  • Playing the instruments

    • - I have a love playing musical instruments, especially the guitar. It’s more than just a hobby for me—playing the guitar helps me relax and express myself through music. Whether I’m playing a simple melody or learning a new song, it brings me a lot of joy. Music has a way of connecting people, and I enjoy sharing that experience with others. Playing the guitar gives me a sense of achievement and allows me to explore different styles of music.

  • Playing Computer Games

    • - In my free time, I like to play computer games such as Far Cry 5 and Valorant. These games offer a way for me to relax and have fun, while also challenging my mind. I enjoy games that make me think and plan, especially when I’m playing with friends or other players online. Games like Far Cry 5 allow me to explore exciting, open-world environments, while Valorant lets me improve my focus and teamwork skills. It’s a fun way to unwind while keeping my brain active.

  • Creating Graphic Designs

    • - Graphic design is something I’m really passionate about because it allows me to be creative and express my thoughts visually. I enjoy working with different design tools to create logos, posters, and other digital artwork. It’s a great way for me to turn my ideas into something real that others can see and appreciate. I love experimenting with colors, shapes, and fonts to create designs that are both eye-catching and meaningful. Designing gives me a sense of accomplishment, especially when people like the work I’ve done.
